Turn any marked-up PDF
into a randomized test.

Drop in your study PDF where the correct answers are already highlighted or coloured. The app reads the file, finds the marked answers, shuffles everything, and gives you a fresh test every time.

How it works

  1. Upload a PDF where each correct answer is highlighted (any colour) or coloured text.
  2. The app parses every question and detects which option is marked.
  3. Choose how many questions you want and start the randomized test.
  4. Get scored, review your mistakes, and retake with a different shuffle.
